About Next Draft

Next Draft was built by a high school student for high school students — because no one understands the pressure of college applications better than someone going through it too.

I’ve reviewed dozens of essays, helped peers find their story, and made sure their writing reflects who they are — clearly, creatively, and confidently. Whether you’re just starting your Common App or polishing your final draft, I’m here to help you put your best words forward.
